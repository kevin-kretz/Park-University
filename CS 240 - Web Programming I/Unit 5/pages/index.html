What are Raspberry Pi and Arduinos?

An Arduino board is a micro-controller board that allows you to compute and controller devices at a very affordable price. A Raspberry Pi is essentially the same thing, just with in the form of a small computer. It has a processor, memory, storage and other parts, just as a normal computer does. Most people prefer to use a Raspberry Pi due to its ease of use based on their previous knowledge of how to use computers.

Why are Raspberry Pi and Arduinos important?

Raspberry Pi and Arduino boards are important becuase they allow anyone to add technology to projects they are working on. For example, you can insert a Raspberry Pi into a Nerf gun and then use the Nerf gun to control a virtual reality headset. This would give you an increadabily realistic feeling for your shooting games. You could also add an arduino board to a small robotics shapes kit and turn a bunch of metal pieces into a working robot. There are endless possibilities for what you can control, all it takes is a little imagination.
